A Domain Specific Language for Accounting

The inception of this research was fueled by the conspicuous absence of a programming language specifically designed to cater to the nuanced tasks and challenges encountered by accounting professionals. My comprehensive research, enriched with the insights and experiences of accounting professionals, is instrumental in illuminating the path to the inception of an accounting domain-specific language (DSL).

Key Objectives:

  • To understand the intrinsic needs and challenges of accounting professionals regarding programming.
  • To analyze and interpret the survey data to extract actionable insights.
  • To design an accounting DSL, tailored to enhance efficiency, accuracy, and productivity in accounting tasks.

Current Progress:

Participate in our detailed survey and contribute your expertise to this research. The survey data is currently under meticulous analysis. Preliminary insights indicate a palpable enthusiasm and need for a specialized programming tool among accounting professionals. Stay tuned for detailed findings, insights, and the unveiling of the next steps towards the realization of the Accounting DSL.
